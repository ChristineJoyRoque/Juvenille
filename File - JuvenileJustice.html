R.A No. 9344 Juvenile Justice and Welfare Act of 2006

Definition and Terms

"Child refers to a person under the age of eighteen (18) years.)"

"Child at Risk" refers to a child who is vulnerable to and at the risk of committing criminal offenses because of personal, family and social circumstances, such as, but not limited to,the following

  • being abuse by any person through sexual, physical, psychological,mental,and economic
  • being exploited, including sexually or economically
  • being abandoned or neglected
  • coming from broken family or without guardian
  • being out of school, being street child, member of gang
  • living in situations of armed conflict
